Ashoka Stack'd

We are a website trying to reduce the book costs faced by students in their day to day college lives. Through the website, you can:

  • Buy
  • Sell
  • Rent In
  • Rent Out

Instead of telling you what price you ought to sell your books at, we let you set a suitable price yourself, so that you decide what the best deal is! So, go ahead try to buy/sell/rent a book yourself!

You can purchase and sell any genre of book, whether it be that incredibly expensive economics textbook or the latest book by J.K. Rowling you've been waiting to gey your hands (but your pocketmoney was running a little low). No hassles, we got you covered. What's more, you can form your own network of people you want to make friends with and start the process with them. We always display search results in the order of you having the book, your friends having the book and then the rest of the stack'd-sphere having the book. So it's always your most trusted first before you move on to others. So, if you find yourself convinced by what we're offering, go ahead and give us a try! #stackd
