What can I do on Stack'd?

Remember that book you wanted but couldn't buy because it was too expensive? Or that paper that didn't go to well because you were short on notes ? Stack'd gives you the opportunity to buy it from sellers at a super-low price!

  • Search for the book or notes you want
  • It's all within Ashoka--created to give you a smoother, more trustworthy transaction.
  • Place your bid!
  • Chat with your seller by email and arrange for a time and place for the exchange and payment.

Stack'd provides you with the platform to sell your books and notes for a good price to those you'd trust--all within campus.

  • Add your book and notes manually in the 'add to bookshelf' section or search for the book in our database
  • Set your own price
  • Find buyers within our database of users within Ashoka.
  • Chat with your buyer through email and arrange for a time and place for the exchange and payment.

Don't like the permanency associated with buying or selling? Try out our rent in/rent out feature!

  • Add your book manually in the 'add book' section or search for the book in our database
  • Set your own price
  • Find buyers by searching within our database of users.
  • Chat with your buyer email and arrange for a time and place for the exchange and payment.
